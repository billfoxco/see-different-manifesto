# The 13 Patterns

These are not principles to follow.
They are patterns that become visible
when perception shifts.

1. Awareness
2. Presence
3. Listening
4. Belief
5. Subtraction
6. Openness
7. Pause
8. Seeing Differently
9. Trust
10. Character
11. Doorway
12. Technology
13. Pattern of Patterns

Each pattern points beyond itself.
